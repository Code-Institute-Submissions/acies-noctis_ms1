About Acies

Acies Noctis is electronic music artist coming from south region of Croatia.Acies begun his musical journey in 2013 under name Shandor. His musical expression is influeced by modern electronic music trends and traditional Dinaric music from south of Croatia. He created music in various genres from dub, trip-hop, drum'n'bass and goatrance and is still performing occasionally under name Shandor, but his main artistic interest in last 4 years is creating dark, pyschedelic trance influenced by Dinaric tradition.
