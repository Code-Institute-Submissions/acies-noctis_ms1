Bio

Acies Noctis is electronic music artist coming from south region of Croatia. His musical expression is influeced by modern electronic music trends and traditional Dinaric music from south of Croatia. Acies begun his musical journey in 2013 under name Shandor. He created music in various genres from dub, trip-hop, drum'n'bass and goatrance.
